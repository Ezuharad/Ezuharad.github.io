About Me

Quality

Quality work is defined by its context; to maximize its utility while minimizing its expense. Thus, the only universally desired quality is efficiency.

Efficiency

To work efficiently is to prioritize, and to prioritize is to let go of perfection. In putting something first one must necessarily put another last.

Adaptability

Ordering of work requires an understanding of evolving needs. Adaptation is then the communication of and adjustment to the context of work.
